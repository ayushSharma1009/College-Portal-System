Facilities in our Institution

TRAMEIT has a well stocked modern library with the total books of all the engineering disciplines. The library is kept open for all working days to inculcate habit of reading and acquing knowledge.

TRAMIET is the proud owner of modern computer centre and a campus wide internet facility with vide band dedicated RF link-32 MBPS. The entire campus in connected with LAN.

Specious and ventilated class rooms for all batches.

A canteen facility is also available in the campus with vide range of services. Canteen provides all daily requirements of the students on payment basis. It remains open 24 hours on working as well as non working days. It serves good quality, hygienic food, snakes, tea, coffee, soup and other soft drinks.

The TRAMIET provides 3 hostels for its students (2 hostels for boys and one for girls). All the rooms are two seater and provide good comfort level to the students. Hot water and cold water supply is available to seasonal needs. Common room facilities with indoor games like table-tennis, chess, carom etc and television set with cable connection. Well equipped modern mess facility to provide nourshigly and quality food to the resident boys and girls separately. The hostel rooms are well furnished with bed, study table, chairs and four etc.

TRAMIET has its own in house dispensary with the residents doctor to attend medical needs to its students. The dispensary is kept open for 24 hours has on all working days. TRAMIET has also its own Ambulance which is available for 24 hours for the students.

The Institute has a fleet of luxury buses for its students. All the buses provide good and relaxed travelling to the students from their respective stations to the college. The Institute has a fleet of 16 buses which bring the students from Mandi, Sunder Nagar, Meramasit, Ner Chowk, Chail Chowk etc, its covers 25 kms from the college.

TRAMIET has its strong Training & Placement cell to:

  • Assist students to develop/clarity their, academic and career interests.
  • Assistant students to develop and implement successful job search strategies.
  • Act as link between students, alumni and the employment community.
  • Assisting difference companies in recruiting candidates as per their requirements.
  • Assisting our students in obtaining final placement in reputed companies.
