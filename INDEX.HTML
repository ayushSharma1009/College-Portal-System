WELCOME TO T.R. ABHILASHI MEMORIAL INSTITUE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

PATH OF EXCELLENCE

Looking at the dearth and demand in the field of higher professional and quality education the foundation of Abhilashi Group of Institutions was laid down on the auspicious day of 21st May 2001. T.R. Abhilashi Memorial Institute of Engineering & Technology is one of the technical campuses of Abhilashi Group of Institutions.
The mission of T.R. Abhilashi Memorial Institute of Engineering & Technology is to advance knowledge and educate students in the field of engineering and technology that will best serve the nation and the world.
T.R. Abhilashi Memorial Institute of Engineering & Technology, Tanda, P.O. Balt, Distt Mandi (H.P.) establishment in May 2009, is a self financing Institute under the registered trust Genius Educational Society.
The Institute is approved by H.P. Govt. and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) , New Delhi and is affiliated to H.P Technical University, Hamirpur.
T.R. Abhilashi Memorial Institute of Engineering & Technology library has thousand of books and also DELNET facility which provides thousand of referred online journals.
T.R. Abhilashi Memorial Institute of Engineering & Technology has separate hostel facility for boys and girls with nygenic mess and aerated rooms.
